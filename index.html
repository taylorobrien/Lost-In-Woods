Strange Call

  • High Concept: You are trying to get out of the woods with out going insane.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should get startled  and feel suspense due to the music.
  • Genre: Horror
  • Game play: In this prototype of In the Woods, the player uses the arrows to move around. The player's objective is to get out of the woods with out dying. They need car keys found inside the house in order to get the car to start so they can leave.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating states, large tilemaps, different light settings, loading state, multiple levels, health bars, sound and using many objects that collide with the tilemap.
  • Features: The effects and sound
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in the middle of the woods.
  • Story: The player went camping with friends and woke up and no one else was there. They must find out how to get home without dying or going insane.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for gamers that enjoy suspense and horror
  • Play time: 3-5 minutes
  • Strategies: Collect all of the keys and get back to the car
