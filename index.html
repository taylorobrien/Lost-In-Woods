Strange Call

  • High Concept: You are trying help the CIA get map pieces, and get out without dying.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should find the game tough, especially in the second level where there is little light.
  • Genre: Side Scroller
  • Game play:In this prototype of Strange Call, the player uses the arrows to move around, including the up arrow to jump. The player's objective is to get the five map pieces located throughout the two levels and get to the emergency door before dying.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating states, large tilemaps, different light settings, loading state, multiple levels, and using many objects that collide with the tilemap.
  • Features: The second level.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is underground, in a dungeon like area.
  • Story: The player is an ex-CIA agent. They find a cellphone ringing in the park, and picks it up to see who it belongs to. When the player answers, it was a call from the CIA, needing their help nearby. The CIA transports the player to where the maps are located.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for gamers that enjoy a challenge.
  • Play time: 2-8 minutes
  • Strategies: Collect all of the coins and avoid the slimes!
